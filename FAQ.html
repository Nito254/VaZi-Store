Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VaZi

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

+

Domestic shipping typically takes 3-5 business days.

How can I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Simply initiate a return through your account or contact our customer service team.

When will I get my refund?

+

Once we receive your return, we'll process the refund within 3-5 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. Your bank may take additional 3-5 business days to post the refund.

How do I exchange an item?

+

For exchanges, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired size/color. This ensures you get your new item as quickly as possible.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my size?

+

Check our detailed size guide on each product page. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing with our size chart. If you're between sizes, consider your preferred fit - size up for relaxed, size down for fitted.

Are your clothes true to size?

+

Most VaZi items run true to size based on standard US sizing. However, some styles may have specific fit notes - these are always mentioned in the product description.

Product Care

How should I wash my VaZi clothes?

+

Always check the care label inside each garment. Generally, we recommend washing in cold water and hanging to dry to maintain quality and extend the life of your clothes.

Do you offer sustainable products?

+

Yes! We're committed to sustainability. Many of our items use organic cotton, recycled materials, or eco-friendly production processes. Look for the "Sustainable" tag on product pages.

Account & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), airtel money and M-pesa for payments.

Is my payment information secure?

+

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data. We never store your full payment details on our servers.

Contact & Support

How can I contact customer service?

+

You can reach us via email at support@vazi.com, through our contact form, or via live chat Monday-Friday 9am-6pm EST. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

Do you have a physical store?

+

Currently, VaZi operates exclusively online. This allows us to offer competitive prices and serve customers worldwide. Follow us on social media for pop-up shop announcements!

Still have questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.
