Joy Muthomi

Director of Creative Direction & Brand Identity

Joy Muthomi is the visionary force behind the aesthetic evolution of VaZi. With a keen eye for contemporary African silhouettes and a passion for sustainable textiles, she oversees the transition from initial sketch to the final runway-ready collection.

Before stepping into her role at VaZi, Joy spent several years collaborating with independent designers across Nairobi, where she gained a reputation for blending traditional craftsmanship with modern streetwear influences. She believes that clothing is not just apparel, but a medium for personal storytelling.

At VaZi, Joy’s leadership ensures that every piece reflects our commitment to quality and cultural relevance. She works closely with our production teams to source ethically made fabrics, ensuring that the brand remains at the forefront of the conscious fashion movement.

Education

  • BA in Fashion Design & Marketing, Mcensal School of Fashion
  • Diploma in Textile Science & Design
  • Certificate in Luxury Brand Management

Areas of Expertise

  • Trend Forecasting & Mood Boarding
  • Sustainable Fabric Sourcing
  • Garment Construction & Technical Design
  • Visual Merchandising
