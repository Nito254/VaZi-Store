Director Profile

Derek

Director of Technology & Cybersecurity

Derek is a Software Engineer and cybersecurity specialist passionate about building secure, scalable, and intelligent digital systems. With a strong technical foundation in software development, systems architecture, and security engineering, he brings both innovation and discipline to every project he leads.

As Director of Technology at VaZi, Derek oversees the company’s technical strategy, infrastructure design, and cybersecurity framework. He is responsible for ensuring that all digital platforms are resilient, efficient, and aligned with VaZi’s long-term vision. His leadership focuses on combining technical excellence with practical execution.

Derek has experience working on complex systems including API integrations, database design, intelligent systems, and secure application architecture. His expertise spans backend development, web technologies, data structures, and security analysis — allowing him to approach challenges from both a development and risk perspective.

Beyond technical execution, Derek believes in continuous growth, innovation, and strategic thinking. His philosophy centers on building technology that not only works — but lasts, scales, and protects. He is committed to driving VaZi forward through disciplined engineering and forward-looking cybersecurity practices.
