Director

With over 15 years of experience in the clothing and fashion industry, Paul has built a reputation for creating innovative and culturally inspired designs. His career began with hands-on work in cloth selling, which provided a deep understanding of fabrics, trends, and customer preferences, forming a solid foundation for his design expertise.

Currently serving as a Member of the Board of Directors at VaZi, Paul plays a pivotal role in shaping strategic decisions and guiding key organizational initiatives. In this leadership position, he ensures that the company's creative and operational goals are aligned, driving both innovation and growth within the organization.

Paul's expertise extends beyond management into high-profile design projects. He worked as a designer for the former president for five years, showcasing his skill in creating distinguished attire. Additionally, Paul has designed outfits for major events such as the last Mulima Festival and the Mr. Medi Competition, combining cultural insight with contemporary fashion trends. Academically, he holds a degree in Cultural Studies in Fashion and software engineering from Kirinyaga University, and professionally, he also brings knowledge as a data scientist, bridging creativity with technology.

Beyond his professional accomplishments, Paul is passionate about integrating culture and innovation in fashion, striving to inspire the next generation of designers while maintaining a strong connection to his roots. His approach reflects a blend of artistry, strategic thinking, and technical proficiency, making him a versatile leader in both the creative and analytical domains.
